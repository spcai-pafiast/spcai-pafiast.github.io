Postdoctoral Research Fellow

Postdoctoral Research Fellow positions in the areas of advanced computer architecture and distributed storage are available immediately in the Gnosis Research Center (GRC) at the Illinois Tech. Candidates with knowledge in memory systems, parallel processing, distributed/parallel I/O, and high-performance computing storage will be preferred. A successful candidate must have a Ph.D. in computer science/engineering, or related area, have an in-depth understanding of computer architecture and distributed storage with a proven capacity for research. The compensation is competitive.

The GRC has received multiple NSF grants and has partners with multiple instutions across the US. Please check our active projects.

The Department of Computer Science at IIT offers B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science and has research strengths in high performance computing (HPC), distributed systems, data science, and algorithms. Based on the CS ranking in College Factual, the department's HPC program is ranked No. 15 in the nation. The department has strong connections to Argonne National Laboratory, Fermi National Laboratory, and to local industry. IIT, located within 10 minutes of downtown Chicago, is a dynamic and innovative institution.
